# Code of Conduct

IndopenSource is a public collaboration space. Contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still naming problems clearly.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.
- Make room for new contributors and different experience levels.
- Avoid harassment, insults, discriminatory language, or repeated unwanted contact.

Maintainers may moderate issues, discussions, pull requests, and other community
spaces to keep collaboration healthy and useful.

Report conduct concerns through the contact channels listed in `SECURITY.md` or
through trusted IndopenSource maintainers.
